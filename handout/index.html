Moonshae Isles Campaign Overview

Welcome to the Moonshae Isles

The Moonshae Isles are a mystical and untamed archipelago steeped in fey magic and Celtic-inspired lore. Prepare for a journey through verdant forests, rugged coastlines, and enigmatic ruins.

Key Features of the Isles

  • Geography: Rolling green hills, dense forests, rocky cliffs, and the ever-present influence of the Earthmother.
  • Factions: The Druids of the Earthmother, Fey Courts, pirates, and opportunistic raiders.
  • Fey Magic: Mysterious portals to the Feywild, bargains with fey creatures, and unpredictable magical phenomena.

An illustration of a famous artifact from the Moonshae Isles

About This Campaign

This campaign will focus on exploration, discovery, and solving mysteries. Expect encounters with fey beings, intricate cryptographic puzzles, and moral choices. Combat will be challenging, requiring strategic thinking and cooperation. Players are encouraged to engage with the world and its inhabitants, as well as each other. Your choices will shape the story and the fate of the Moonshae Isles.

Your Party's Story

Fresh off of your party's success against the dragon in Waterdeep you find yourselves down a member. While in the Vault your companion, the Wizard Eryndor Valin, believes he discovered some hint toward the location of an ancient artifact that his family has spent much of their history searching for. During the celebration following a succesful heist you noticed Eryndor hard at work, buried in books away from everyone. The following morning, tankards dry and throughts swimming you find the corner he occupied empty save for a single note scrawled on the back of a page torn from one of his books. The page contains an illustration of an angular black obelisk with seemingliy incoherent symbols carved into the surface. The script on the front side of the page is written in Fey, however the writing on the back is in Common and says "I've found the way among the Isle's of Moonshae" There is also a glyph drawn beneath each "E" in the message (See Handout 1).

Starting Equipment

Each character will start at level 5 with 10,000gp plus one rare or magical item rolled from the table appropriate to your class (DM See Appendix A) Your benefactor has promised you passage to the Moonshae Isle's, so you need not worry about a ship. Session one will pick up at your benefactors home after you have had the chance to spend your rewards; so come prepared with a full tool belt and empty pockets!

Prepare to delve into the mysteries of the Moonshae Isles. May the Earthmother guide your path.
